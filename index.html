The Real Anytown

knows your order.

The Local isn't trying to be something it's not. It's the bar where regulars have a stool, bartenders know your name before you sit down, and the drinks hit exactly as hard as they're supposed to.

Voted #1 by the people who actually drink here. Karaoke nights that get out of hand in the best way. Pool tables that see real competition. Live music that fills every inch of the room.

If you want craft cocktails in a quiet corner, the internet has 40 suggestions. If you want cold beer, a Bloody Mary made right, and a bartender who actually talks to you — pull up a stool.
